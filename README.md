# 180 Degrees Consulting – Business Consulting & Social Impact Projects

> Consulting Projects | 180 Degrees Consulting, NIT Warangal

## Overview

This repository showcases consulting projects completed as a member of **180 Degrees Consulting, NIT Warangal**, where multidisciplinary teams collaborated with social enterprises and organizations to solve real-world challenges through research, data analytics, business strategy, and technology-driven solutions.

The projects focus on understanding stakeholder needs, analysing operational challenges, and delivering practical, evidence-based recommendations to create measurable social impact.

---

# Projects Included

## 1. LabourHome – Workforce Analytics & Social Impact Assessment

LabourHome is a technology-enabled platform connecting blue-collar workers with employment opportunities.

### Project Objectives

- Analyse workforce demographics and employment patterns
- Identify challenges faced by blue-collar workers
- Study migration, wage expectations, and employment stability
- Generate data-driven recommendations for improving workforce engagement
- Support strategic decision-making through quantitative analysis

### Key Contributions

- Conducted primary research involving **2,000+ blue-collar workers**
- Processed and analysed large survey datasets
- Identified workforce trends and operational challenges
- Developed analytical reports and strategic recommendations
- Contributed to client presentations and impact assessment

---

## 2. EcoSaviours – Sustainable Community Engagement Platform

EcoSaviours is an initiative focused on promoting environmental sustainability through digital solutions for waste management, cleanliness drives, and community participation.

### Project Objectives

- Improve citizen engagement in environmental initiatives
- Design technology-driven sustainability solutions
- Develop strategies for waste management and tree monitoring
- Encourage community participation through digital platforms

### Key Contributions

- Analysed environmental management workflows
- Proposed technology-enabled community engagement strategies
- Evaluated operational bottlenecks and improvement opportunities
- Developed solution frameworks supporting sustainable urban development
